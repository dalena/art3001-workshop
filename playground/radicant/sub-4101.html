Bicycle by Ziying Ye

"I spent most of my childhood with my grandmother since my parents were busy working. My grandmother was my "driver". She used an old bicycle to send for me and to pick me up as I sat behind her. Life was simple and easy at that time. All the details might fade out through time, but those emotions and feelings will be kept forever. The work is dedicated to my beloved grandma, the most important person in my life."
