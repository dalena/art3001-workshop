ART 3001 - Internet Art

w/ Instructor Dalena Tran & Hirad Sab

"Energize" by Sara Miskus

This interactive website explores the personality traits of introverts, extroverts, and ambiverts.

Visit "Energize"

"TwoCities" by Christian J. Harris & Richard Giang

The city isn't always as it seems. A city can have two sides. One pristine and one only seen by some. Columbus, Ohio is no different.

Visit "Twocities"

"Black Voices at Ohio State" by Richard Giang

A landing page platform for the news articles published featuring prominent Black members of the Ohio State community in a project titled "Black Voices at Ohio State". This project was a collaboration between Black and Bold Magazine, the premiere Black student publication at Ohio State, and the Lantern, the independent student voice of Ohio State.

Visit "Black Voices at Ohio State"

"Rattack" by Lilly Rakas

The only objective in this simple browser-based game is to cause havoc. Move your mouse to control the rat. Click on the various items to Rattack them. Click reset to start again.

Visit "Rattack"

"Which is Witch?" by Zehur Elmi

Well? Read closely and find out.

Visit "Which is Witch?"

"Collage Maker" by Nataly Vergara

Create you own custom collage. Images are an archive of student contributions in ART 3001 curated and coded by Nataly Vergara.

Visit "Collage Maker"

"Napateccas" by Lucia Perfumo

Napateccas is a town under the ground for all different types of creatures. You are moving into this new town but something doesn’t feel quite right.

Visit "Napateccas"

"Mime Prototype" by Sydney Kit

This is a prototype of a video game that has been in the works for two years. Artwork by Sydney Kit.

Visit "Mime Prototype"

"The Last Duel" by Zehur Elmi

Visit "The Last Duel"

"Ari Run" by Heesoo Lee

This is a simple interactive project where internet art has merged with my love for my dog. The user can scroll to see my dog, Ari, run as he takes you on a short journey of where he has been throughout his life.

Visit "Ari Run"

"Playground" by Madalyn Bunjevac

Come have a look at all the different places you could be

Visit "Playground"

"Pop on a Shelf" by Gary Isufi & Heesoo Lee

This work allows the user to manipulate each individual Funko Pop's location on a bookshelf. It serves as a rough draft for our future living space's wall decor.

Visit "Funko Feng Shui"

"Creature Maker" by Kendall Mabee

Customize your own creature by clicking on each element of the face. Original artwork by Kendall Mabee

Visit "Creature Maker"

"The Paperback Riders" by Sara Miskus

"The Paperback Riders" is the name for a made-up book club centered around those who love reading fiction. This website was focused around developing an imaginary community.

Visit "The Paperback Riders"

"The Garden" by Madison Shimborske

"The Garden" is an interactive choose-your-own-adventure experience where the choices you make affect whether you make it out alive! In this garden, you may experience mythical beings, mysterious doors, treacherous curses, and a whole lot of magic. Watch your step and try to make it through the garden!

Visit "The Garden"

"Random Acts of Kindness" by Lucia Perfumo

"Random Acts of Kindness" is an organization that provides resources for schools to create a positive environment for students.

Visit "Random Acts of Kindness"

"PRIDE" by Zachary Upperman

This website is meant to be used as a safe-haven for all people alike to come and either learn about the LGBTQ community or become part of it.

Visit "PRIDE"
